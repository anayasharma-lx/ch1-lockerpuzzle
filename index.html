Open lockers — all perfect squares

Why? Only square numbers have an odd number of factors. Every other number's factors come in distinct pairs (e.g. 1×6 and 2×3 for locker 6), giving an even toggle count. But a square number has one factor that is its own partner (e.g. 6×6=36), making the total count odd — so the locker stays open.
